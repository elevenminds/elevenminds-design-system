What stays elevenminds

The skeleton beneath every sub-brand

The platform

Same backend, same data model, same APIs. A fleet, geocerca, ruta and unidad mean the same thing across NAANIX and SphereGT.

Components & conventions

Tables, alert states, map controls, settings panes, popups all share structure and behavior. Each sub-system restyles them, never re-invents.

The icon library

One symbol set. Sub-systems may change the container (filled circle vs. outline) and accent color, but the glyphs remain elevenminds.

Engineering

One codebase, theme-switched at the distributor level. New sub-systems are a tokens.css + asset pack — never a fork.

"Powered by elevenminds"

Every sub-brand acknowledges the platform — usually as an eyebrow or footer attribution. Discreet, but always present.

Spanish-first content

All sub-systems serve Mexican operators. Voice differs by audience, but Spanish is the source language — English is the translation, not the other way around.
